Snapfacture is a minimalist invoicing app for tradespeople, freelancers and one-person businesses paid on the spot (cash, card, instant transfer).

<b>Issue a legal invoice in about 20 seconds</b>: tap your products, charge, share the PDF by email or messaging before your customer walks out.

<b>Features</b>
- Few-tap invoice creation from your product catalog
- Credit notes in one tap
- Gapless sequential numbering and immutable issued invoices
- Verifiable SHA-256 anti-fraud audit chain (French profile)
- PDF generation and sharing, fully offline
- Country profiles: France (VAT, statutory B2B mentions, franchise regime) and United States (sales tax)
- Revenue dashboard and tax summary
- CSV export for your accountant
- Manual or automatic backup to a folder you choose (Drive, OneDrive, SD card…)
- Optional biometric lock

<b>Private by design</b>: no account, no server, no analytics, and no network permission at all — your invoices and customers never leave your phone unless you export them yourself.
